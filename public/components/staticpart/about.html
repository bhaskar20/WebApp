WHO WE ARE

Founded by an MBA graduate and 3 IIT-Bombay Alumni, LogiPort is Technology Based Logistics platform with a sole purpose of ruthlessly reduce inefficiencies in the commercial vehicle transportation space by applying technology and big data analytics


WHAT WE DO

We provide Intelligent Transportation Solutions to our clients that primarily address 4 key areas of a fleet operations

1.Real Time tracking of fleet with complete geo-visualization, Load status, and Driver status
2.Providing routing and dispatch management.
3. We provide unmatched delivery timings with the help of our healthy technology-based transportation Ecosystem and unique driver Engagements
4.Enable real-time communications, not just with the driver, but with truck and cargo monitoring systems with alerts when prescribed parameters are exceeded.

WHY US?

At LogiPort we are enabling logistics with technology and re-invented it, thus making it efficient and sustainable. We first seek to understand our clients needs and establish a baseline on which to operate and provide a technology platform to fulfill that need with reliability while improving operational efficiency such as driver engagement and on the ground quick response.
